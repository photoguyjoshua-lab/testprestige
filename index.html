How We Support You

01

Procurement Advisory & Route-to-Market Support

We support organisations in developing robust procurement strategies that align with corporate objectives. This includes spend analysis, category management support, pipeline planning, route-to-market advice, and market assessment to help inform effective procurement decisions.

02

Tender Documentation & Procurement Administration

From drafting specifications, RFQs, ITTs, and evaluation criteria to supporting procurement portal administration and notice preparation, we help ensure procurements are well-documented, compliant, and transparent. All approvals and award decisions remain with the client.

03

Evaluation Support & Assurance

We design evaluation models and scoring frameworks, provide guidance for client evaluation panels, conduct moderation and consistency reviews, and prepare assurance reporting to strengthen the defensibility of procurement decisions. All scoring and ranking is undertaken by the client.

04

Contract & Commissioning Support

Our support encompasses needs assessment, options appraisals, commissioning strategy development, contract management frameworks, KPI development, and supplier performance review support. We bring an evidence-led approach to help design services that meet community and organisational needs.

05

Governance, Risk & Compliance Reviews

We provide procurement policy and process reviews, governance and assurance assessments, conflict of interest control reviews, audit readiness assessments, risk identification and mitigation advice, and procurement legislation compliance advice, including compliance with the Procurement Act 2023.

06

Training & Capability Development

We deliver tailored training programmes for procurement professionals, commissioners, and senior decision-makers. From legislation updates and evaluation best practice to procurement fundamentals and advanced contract strategy, we build capability that lasts. Our courses are available as bespoke in-house sessions. Structured online courses are in development and will be available via Udemy and direct through our website ahead of our July 2026 launch.

Our
Background

Prestige Commercial Consulting was founded on the belief that public sector procurement deserves the same rigour, expertise, and strategic thinking as the private sector, delivered with the transparency and ethical commitment that public service demands.

Our directors bring over a decade of combined experience in senior public sector procurement and commissioning roles, having worked across local government and the wider public sector. Prior to founding Prestige, they held operational and strategic procurement positions supporting programmes spanning construction, professional services, IT, and social care — with public contracts with aggregate values exceeding £250 million, spanning our directors' combined public sector careers. That hands-on practitioner background is what distinguishes Prestige: we have sat in the roles our clients hold, and we understand the pressures, governance requirements, and practical realities they face.

The client retains ownership of all decisions and approvals throughout. Our success is measured by yours: compliant processes, better outcomes, and strengthened capability.
